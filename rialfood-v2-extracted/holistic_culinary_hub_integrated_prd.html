# Holistic Culinary Hub (Integrated) ## Product Overview **The Pitch:** A unified culinary platform combining nutritional tracking, recipe curation, and biometric-style journaling into a gamified editorial experience. It solves engagement drop-off by bridging the gap between what you eat, how you feel, and the community you share it with. **For:** Fitness enthusiasts tracking macros, meticulous meal planners, and food creators looking to monetize and engage their audience. **Device:** desktop **Design Direction:** Notion meets Kinfolk magazine. Editorial, clean, minimalist, featuring earth tones, deliberate whitespace, and elegant serif typography. **Inspired by:** Notion, Strava, Oura/Whoop (for data visualization). --- ## Screens - **The Daily Hub (Dashboard):** High-level view of today's meals, energy scores, and quick logging. - **Vitals & Food Diary:** Granular logging of meals paired with emotional and physical states. - **Recipe Vault:** Visual, Pinterest-style repository for scraped IG/YouTube links and original recipes. - **Smart Weekly Planner:** Drag-and-drop meal calendar integrated with an automated grocery list. - **Community Feed:** Strava-style timeline showing friends' culinary achievements and creator content. --- ## Key Flows **Logging a Meal & Vitals:** Connect food intake with physical well-being. 1. User is on The Daily Hub -> sees **Log Today's Energy & Meal** CTA. 2. User clicks CTA -> opens side-panel with slider for energy/digestion and search for recipe/ingredients. 3. Form submits -> Updates daily 'Vitality Score' and awards 50 EXP points. **Saving and Planning a Recipe:** Curate external content and plan for the week. 1. User is on Recipe Vault -> clicks **Paste Link**. 2. User pastes an Instagram URL -> system extracts thumbnail, title, and tags. 3. User drags the new recipe card into the **Smart Weekly Planner** -> Auto-generates missing ingredients in the grocery list. ---
Design System ## Color Palette - **Primary:** `#4A5D23` - Olive green for primary buttons, active states, progress bars. - **Background:** `#F9F8F6` - Warm oat, entirely replacing stark white for the main canvas. - **Surface:** `#FFFFFF` - Cards, modals, floating menus. - **Text:** `#2B2927` - Deep espresso for maximum readability without harshness. - **Muted:** `#A39F98` - Warm gray for secondary text, subtle borders, inactive states. - **Accent:** `#D47A43` - Terracotta for alerts, badges, notifications, and 'Chef Pro' highlights. ## Typography Choose fonts that are **distinctive and characterful**, bringing an editorial, print-like quality. - **Headings:** `Newsreader`, 400 italic / 600, 24-48px (Serif, elegant, literary) - **Body:** `Satoshi`, 400, 16px (Clean, geometric sans-serif, high legibility) - **Small text:** `Satoshi`, 500, 13px, uppercase tracking - **Buttons:** `Satoshi`, 700, 14px **Style notes:** 0px border radius on internal elements, 4px on main containers. 1px solid borders (`#E5E3DF`) instead of drop shadows. Layout relies heavily on structural grids, thick dividing lines (2px), and deliberate negative space. ## Design Tokens ```css :root { --color-primary: #4A5D23; --color-background: #F9F8F6; --color-surface: #FFFFFF; --color-text: #2B2927; --color-muted: #A39F98; --color-accent: #D47A43; --font-heading: 'Newsreader', serif; --font-body: 'Satoshi', sans-serif; --radius-sm: 0px; --radius-md: 4px; --spacing-base: 8px; --border-subtle: 1px solid #E5E3DF; --border-bold: 2px solid #2B2927; } ```

Screen Specifications ### The Daily Hub **Purpose:** Command center detailing the day's nutritional roadmap and biometric readiness. **Layout:** 3-column split. Left: Navigation. Center (60%): Today's Meals & Vitals. Right (20%): Gamification & Stats. **Key Elements:** - **Vitality Score Ring:** `200px` circular chart, `#4A5D23` fill, merging sleep, digestion, and macro completion. - **Today's Itinerary:** Vertical timeline of Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks. - **Creator Badge:** Top right corner, displaying current level (e.g., `Lvl 12 • Sous Chef`). **States:** - **Empty:** "No meals logged yet. Start your day off right." with a stark, outline-only CTA. - **Loading:** Skeleton lines matching exact typography line-heights. - **Error:** Subtle inline text `#D47A43`, "Unable to sync vitals." **Components:** - **Meal Card:** `100% width`, `Surface`, `Border-subtle`, containing thumbnail, title, macros, and `Edit` icon. - **Log Button:** `Primary` background, `Surface` text, flat design, no shadow. **Interactions:** - **Click [Meal Card]:** Expands in-place to reveal recipe details and ingredient checklist. - **Hover [Log Button]:** Background shifts to `#3A4A1C`. **Responsive:** - **Desktop:** 3-column grid layout. - **Tablet:** 2 columns (Stats move below Center content). - **Mobile:** Single column, stacked. ### Vitals & Food Diary **Purpose:** Connect physiological data to culinary intake. **Layout:** Two-pane split. Left: List of logged days. Right: Detailed journal entry for the selected day. **Key Elements:** - **Biometric Sliders:** Minimalist horizontal track (`#E5E3DF`) with a square thumb (`#4A5D23`) for Energy, Digestion, Mood. - **Macro Table:** Editorial-style table with bold top border (`2px solid #2B2927`) detailing Proteins, Fats, Carbs. - **Workout Linker:** Dropdown to select the day's physical activity (e.g., "Leg Day", "10k Run"). **States:** - **Empty:** Faded placeholder text encouraging reflection. - **Loading:** Fading pulse on the entire right pane. **Components:** - **Slider Input:** `100% width`, 24px height. - **Table Row:** `16px` padding, 1px bottom border. **Interactions:** - **Drag [Slider]:** Real-time numeric update above the thumb. - **Hover [Table Row]:** Background shifts to `#F5F4F1`. ### Recipe Vault **Purpose:** Organize, filter, and view saved and original recipes visually. **Layout:** Masonry grid with a sticky top filter bar. **Key Elements:** - **Filter Bar:** Horizontal scroll of tags (e.g., `High Protein`, `Batch Cook`, `IG Saves`). - **Recipe Card:** Image-heavy, tall aspect ratio (4:5), title in `Newsreader`, source icon (IG/YT) in top-left corner. - **Add Link Input:** `400px` wide input field in header with a `Extract` button. **States:** - **Empty:** Illustration of an empty recipe box, "Paste a link to begin your collection." - **Loading:** Blurred gray rectangles matching card dimensions. **Components:** - **Tag Pill:** `Border-subtle`, `Radius-sm`, `Satoshi 13px` text. - **Recipe Thumbnail:** 100% width, sharp corners, monochrome filter applied until hover. **Interactions:** - **Hover [Recipe Card]:** Monochrome filter drops, image slightly scales up (1.02x) over 0.3s. - **Click [Extract]:** Input field transforms into a progress bar, then animates a new card falling into the grid. ### Smart Weekly Planner **Purpose:** Orchestrate weekly meals and auto-generate grocery needs. **Layout:** 7-column calendar grid (top 60%) + Grocery List drawer (bottom 40%). **Key Elements:** - **Day Column:** Header with day and date, drop zone for Recipe Cards. - **Batch Cooking Badge:** `#D47A43` pill indicating recipes yielding >4 servings. - **Leftover Warning:** Diagonal striped background on slots automatically filled by a previous day's batch cook. **States:** - **Empty:** Empty drop zones with subtle dashed borders. - **Loading:** Spinner in the center of the calendar. **Components:** - **Mini Recipe Card:** Draggable, `100% width`, `60px height`, text only. - **Grocery Item:** Checkbox + Label + Aisle category tag. **Interactions:** - **Drag & Drop [Mini Card]:** Snaps to Day Column, instantly updates Grocery List drawer below. - **Click [Checkbox]:** Text gets a strikethrough, color shifts to `Muted`. ### Community Feed **Purpose:** Social validation, inspiration, and gamification mechanics. **Layout:** Single central column (600px max-width), aligned left. **Key Elements:** - **Feed Item:** User avatar, action text ("*Elena* logged a 10k run & cooked *Lemon Salmon*"), large photo, like/comment buttons. - **Leaderboard Sidebar:** Sticky left sidebar ranking friends by XP earned this week. - **Chef Pro Badge:** Gold/Terracotta shield icon next to verified creator names. **States:** - **Empty:** "Find friends or creators to follow." - **Loading:** 3 skeleton feed items. **Components:** - **Action Text:** `Newsreader` for user names and recipe titles, `Satoshi` for verbs. - **Like Button:** Flame icon, shifts to `#D47A43` when active. **Interactions:** - **Click [Like]:** Icon pops (1.2x scale) and particles emit. - **Click [Recipe Title]:** Opens modal to instantly save that recipe to user's Recipe Vault.

Build Guide **Stack:** HTML + Tailwind CSS v3 **Build Order:** 1. **The Daily Hub** - Establishes the core layout, background colors, and the critical `Newsreader`/`Satoshi` typographic pairing. 2. **Recipe Vault** - Introduces the masonry grid and image treatment rules (monochrome hover states). 3. **Smart Weekly Planner** - Implements complex drag-and-drop interactions and layout splits. 4. **Vitals & Food Diary** - Adds form controls, sliders, and editorial table designs. 5. **Community Feed** - Reuses components from previous screens to build the social timeline and leaderboards.
