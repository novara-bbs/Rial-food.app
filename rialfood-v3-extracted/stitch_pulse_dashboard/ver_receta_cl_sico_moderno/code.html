Creamy Tuscan Chicken

A modern classic for weeknight dinners featuring a rich garlic parmesan sauce.

Ingredients

Instructions

1

Season chicken with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat and cook chicken until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes per side.

2

Remove chicken and set aside. In the same skillet, add garlic and sun-dried tomatoes. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

3

Stir in heavy cream and parmesan cheese. Bring to a light simmer, then add spinach until wilted. Return chicken to the pan to coat.
