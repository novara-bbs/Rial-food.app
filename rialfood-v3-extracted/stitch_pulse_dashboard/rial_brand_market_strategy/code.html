Color Theory

Vitality Lime HEX #DDFF00

The color of growth and biological energy. Lime cuts through digital noise, representing the "Real Data" aspect—sharp, precise, and active.

Deep Obsidian HEX #121408

Derived from the primary hue but darkened for depth. Obsidian represents the "Real Feel"—premium, sophisticated, and the solid foundation of our data ecosystem.
